About Us

The Southend Linux Users Group (SLUG) is a community-driven organization dedicated to promoting the use of Linux and open-source software in Southend-on-Sea and the surrounding areas. We aim to provide a platform for learning, sharing, and connecting with like-minded individuals.

We hold monthly meetings, workshops, and social events. Join us to learn, share, and connect!
