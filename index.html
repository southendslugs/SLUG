About SLUG

Welcome to the Southend Linux Users Group (SLUG) in Seattle!

We are a community of Linux enthusiasts who meet regularly to share knowledge, discuss projects,

and promote the use of Linux andopen-source software.

Mission:

Our mission is to build a welcoming and dynamic community of Linux enthusiasts,

developers, and professionals. We aim to foster an environment where members can

share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and develop their skills in Linux and open-source

software.

Vision:

Our vision is to be the leading Linux community that inspires and drives innovation and

collaboration. We envision a future where Linux and open-source software are widely

adopted and accessible to all, driven by the contributions and advancements of our

members.

Meetings

Our meetings are held on the first Saturday of every month at 2 PM.

We gather at the Seattle Central Library, located at 1000 4th Ave, Seattle, WA 98104.

Contact

If you have any questions or would like to join our group, please email us at

contact@southendlug.org.
